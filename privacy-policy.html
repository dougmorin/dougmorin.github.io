Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how dougmor.in collects, uses, and protects personal information obtained through automated communications, in accordance with the Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) set forth by Mailgun.

Information Collection:

Personal Information:

We may collect personal information such as names, email addresses, and other relevant details necessary for automated communications.

Automated Communications:

Dougmor.in integrates with Nextcloud, which may involve the collection of personal information for the purpose of facilitating automated emails and communications. This includes, but is not limited to, notifications, alerts, and updates generated by Nextcloud to keep users informed about their account activities, shared files, and other relevant information.

Use of Information:

Communication with Nextcloud:

Personal information collected will be used exclusively for facilitating communication within the Nextcloud platform. This includes, but is not limited to, notifications, updates, and alerts related to account activities, shared files, and other relevant information specific to Nextcloud usage.

Mailgun AUP Compliance:

Dougmor.in adheres to Mailgun's Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) available at https://www.mailgun.com/legal/aup/, ensuring compliance with their guidelines.

Security:

Data Security:

We implement reasonable security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

Third-Party Disclosure:

Mailgun Integration:

Personal information may be shared with Mailgun for the purpose of delivering automated communications, in compliance with their AUP.

Communication Preferences:

Users have the option to manage their communication preferences within their Nextcloud account settings. To opt-out of specific automated communications, please update your preferences accordingly.

Updates to Privacy Policy:

Policy Changes:

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Users will be notified of any significant updates.
